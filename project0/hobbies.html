Hobbies

I have been a keen runner since 10 years old. I was a succesful amateur athlete in the past, winning the Northern UK Championships at 400m hurdles and placing 2nd in the UK junior championships in 2011. Since then I am no longer competing but still enjoy training and the health benefits that come with it.
