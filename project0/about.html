About me

I am from the UK, born in the northern city of York sometime during 1992. I lived their until I was 18 years old when i moved to the University of East Anglia to study my bachelors in Accounting. Three years later and with a thirst to expand my horizons, I moved to Thailand to study my Masters in Finance. Upon graduation I found an English teaching post which sparked a journey through 5 different cities in 2 different countries to where I currently reside in Guanghou, China. I believe I have found my passion in web programming and I am currently studying hard to make this a career.
